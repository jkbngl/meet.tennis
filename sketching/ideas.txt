there are users

users can be in 0 - n clubs
users in clubs can have different roles, they can manage
	- other users roles
	- courts
some roles are:
	- pending approve (requested but is not confirmed)
	- user (approved, can request courts)
	- admin (can manage courts)
	- superadmin (can manage other users)

there are courts
1 court does always have to be in 1 club

courts have schedules
in which they are generally open
and in which they are available to book
